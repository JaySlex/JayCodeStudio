What is it?
Concarneau Force is a kind of GTA, but in this game we follow the story of a young man who wants to be part of the police force.
But if it were only that, the game will be very short.
Fortunately for us and unfortunately for our protagonist, its evolution in the police force will not be without vicissitudes.
In this game, your actions will have an influence on your reputation, it allows you to advance in the game.
So, if you miss a service, if you take your personal phone during your service or you're crazy shit wait for your superiors to be distant!
